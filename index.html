Welcome to ACC 2025

Our Vision

ACC 2025 is a major international conference aimed at fostering collaboration and sharing cutting-edge research to shape the future of cardiovascular medicine in the ASEAN region and beyond.

The Theme

Our theme, "Reimagining ASEAN Cardiology," focuses on innovative technologies, future trends, and addressing the unique cardiovascular health challenges of Southeast Asia.

Networking

Connect with leading cardiologists, surgeons, researchers, and healthcare professionals from around the world in the vibrant city of Bangkok.

Program & Audience

Scientific Program Highlights

  • Plenary lectures from world-renowned experts
  • In-depth symposia and practical workshops
  • Oral and poster presentations of new research
  • Discussions on clinical best practices
  • Sessions on new cardiovascular guidelines

Who Should Attend?

  • Cardiologists (Adult & Pediatric)
  • Cardiac and Vascular Surgeons
  • General Physicians & Practitioners
  • Researchers and Medical Scientists
  • Nurses & Allied Health Professionals
